# Generated Image Usage

Last updated: May 29, 2026

This page provides guidelines for sharing or publishing wallpaper images generated by WondayWall.
These guidelines apply to Windows, iOS, and Android.

## About Generated Images

Wallpapers generated by WondayWall are created by the Google Gemini API (an AI model).
Generated images are not photographs or human-authored creative works; they are content automatically produced by AI.

## Storage, Sharing, and Wallpaper Setup by OS

| OS | Storage, sharing, and wallpaper setup |
|----|---------------------------------------|
| Windows | Generated images are stored by the app and may be applied as the desktop wallpaper. If enabled, they may also be applied to the lock screen |
| iOS | Generated images are stored in the app and may be used for Photos saving, sharing, and manual wallpaper setup. The iOS app does not directly change the home screen or lock screen wallpaper |
| Android | Generated images are stored in the app and may be applied to the home screen or lock screen wallpaper through `WallpaperManager`. They may also be saved to Photos/Gallery or shared |

On every OS, before sharing or publishing an image, check whether it may contain personal information, schedule details, locations, confidential information, or content related to third-party rights.

## Posting to SNS, Blogs, Videos, and Product Pages

When posting or sharing generated images on social media, blogs, videos, product pages, or similar platforms, follow the terms of service and community guidelines of each platform.
If a service has specific rules regarding AI-generated content, ensure you comply with those rules.

## Disclosing AI-Generated Content

Depending on the platform and context, you may be required to disclose that an image was created by AI.
Add a label such as "AI-generated image" where appropriate.

## Real People, Copyrighted Works, and Trademarks

Exercise caution when using or publishing images that may appear to contain:

- Real individuals or public figures
- Content that resembles existing copyrighted works, characters, or artistic styles
- Trademarks, logos, or brand elements

Avoid using such images commercially or in any way that infringes on the rights of third parties.

## Personal and Confidential Information

WondayWall generates wallpapers based on calendar events, RSS news, interest keywords, and additional instructions.
Take care before publishing images that may visually reveal:

- Calendar event details, locations, or names
- Internal business information, project names, or client details
- Personal schedule or location data
- Confidential information entered as additional instructions

Avoid publishing images that could expose such information to the public.

## News and Real-World Events

WondayWall may generate wallpapers based on RSS news feeds.
Generated images are AI-created fiction and are not photographs or factual records of real events.
Even if an image relates to a news story or real-world event, do not treat it as a factual record or share it in a context that could be misleading.

## Commercial Use and Public Distribution

Before using generated images commercially or making them publicly available, verify the following yourself:

- The rights and usage conditions for generated content under the terms of service of the external services used, such as the Google Gemini API
- The terms of service and AI-generated content policies of the platform where you intend to publish
- That the image content does not infringe third-party rights, such as copyright, portrait rights, or trademark rights
- That the image does not contain personal information, schedule details, locations, or confidential information
- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

We are not responsible for the content of generated images, their rights status, or any consequences of publishing them.
